Automatic Metadata Editor for Audio Files: SongCapper

This is SongCapper, a program that iterates through a folder full of music files, reads their metadata, and rewrites it, but this time in all capital letters.

This idea was born while I was a student at the University of Minnesota. I had taken up a side gig as a DJ and, at times, desired to do some freestyle sets. However, since artists will stylize their song titles however they like, large music libraries can be quite hard to read. This can be especially tricky when you need quick song recognition. By homogenizing song titles and artist names to all caps, you can increase the readability of the music library for on-the-go song selection..
